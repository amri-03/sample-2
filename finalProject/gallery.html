Photography Tips for Each Style

Silhouette Photography

Position your subject between you and a bright light source. Expose for the background to create dramatic silhouettes with vibrant colors.

Reflection Photography

Use calm water surfaces to create mirror-like reflections. Shoot at different angles to find the most compelling composition.

Creative Motion Blur

Use slow shutter speeds and intentional camera movement to create abstract, artistic images with flowing lines and colors.
